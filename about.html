About MRABILITY Finance

Redefining How India Borrows

Founded in 2015, MRABILITY Finance connects millions of Indians with the right financial products through technology, transparency, and trust.

Our Story

MRABILITY Finance started with a simple belief — getting a loan in India shouldn't be complicated, opaque, or time-consuming. Our founders experienced firsthand the frustration of navigating dozens of banks, inconsistent interest rates, and mountains of paperwork.

So we built a platform that brings 95+ lenders onto one screen, uses AI to match borrowers with the best offers, and guides every customer from application to disbursal — digitally, transparently, and fast.

Today, MRABILITY Finance serves 5.8 lakh+ customers annually across 150+ cities and has facilitated over ₹61,000 Crore in loan disbursals.
